# Sure Mineral Intelligence

Sure Mineral Intelligence is an AI-powered mineral acquisition intelligence platform.

## Purpose

Identify high-potential mineral opportunities before competitors by combining geological intelligence, license intelligence, operator intelligence, and acquisition prioritization.

## Capabilities

- Geological Intelligence
- License Intelligence
- Operator Intelligence
- Executive Intelligence
- Acquisition Intelligence
- Prospect Ranking
- Drill Target Generation
- Mineral Corridor Analysis
- Acquisition Priority Scoring
